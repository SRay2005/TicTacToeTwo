How to Play

The Board

A 3×3 grid of mini tic-tac-toe boards. Win 3 mini boards in a row to win the game.

The Rule

Where you play inside a mini board determines which mini board your opponent must play in next.

Play in the top-left cell of any board → opponent must play in the top-left board
Free Choice

If the board you're sent to is already won or drawn, you may play in any open board.

Winning

Claim a mini board by getting 3 in a row inside it. Get 3 claimed boards in a row on the outer grid to win.

Yellow border = board you must play in
Faint yellow = any board is valid
